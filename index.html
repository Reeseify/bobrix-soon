Statements regarding the recent “Schlep” situation

Bobrix is not affiliated with Roblox. The following statements are provided to clarify our stance and the design choices we are making in light of recent reporting and public discussion.
Our safety stance
  • No vigilantism on Bobrix. We prohibit decoy accounts and sting operations. They can put minors at risk and contaminate evidence. Instead, we provide built‑in, one‑click law‑enforcement referrals with secure evidence packaging.
  • Hard guardrails against off‑platform lures. Our systems flag attempts to move conversations to external apps, auto‑mute the content, and notify guardians for under‑13 accounts.
  • Independent oversight. An external Safety Council reviews our policies and may audit high‑impact enforcement decisions.
  • Transparency over spin. We will publish a quarterly Safety Report and share de‑identified research with academics and NGOs.
What reportedly happened elsewhere (Aug 2025)

Multiple outlets reported that Roblox removed or banned vigilante creators, including a YouTuber known as “Schlep,” citing terms‑of‑service violations related to impersonation of minors and moving users off‑platform. Lawsuits in several states also alleged inadequate protection of minors on the platform. Roblox has stated it partners with law enforcement and opposes vigilantism, and it has announced policy changes around ratings and access.

Sources noted by the Bobrix team: major news reports (Aug 13–30, 2025) and Roblox’s public newsroom post from Aug 2025.

How Bobrix is different
  • Built‑in evidence handling: report → preserve → escalate, without public exposure or vigilantism.
  • Strict age boundaries: enforced chat & matchmaking separation between minors and adults unless verified family/guardian.
  • Creator obligations: creators must adopt our Safety Toolkit (chat filters, session limits, reporting hooks) to publish.
  • Third‑party audits: regular red‑team exercises and external compliance reviews (e.g., NCMEC alignment, COPPA/UK AADC).

FAQ

When is Bobrix launching?

Target window is late 2025. We’ll invite a small creator cohort first, then expand to families and schools.

How do you handle reports?

Emergency content triggers immediate locks and a 24/7 escalation path. All reports receive a ticket ID and status updates.

What about data privacy?

We practice data minimization. Guardian dashboards can delete minor data, and we avoid dark patterns around consent.

Are you affiliated with Roblox?

No. Bobrix is an independent project and not endorsed by, or connected to, Roblox Corporation.
