Safety by design — and what we learned from the “Schlep” situation

Bobrix is not affiliated with Roblox. The Schlep controversy was a catalyst for us — it reinforced our decision to build Bobrix and to formalize safe reporting channels for the community.
Our stance
  • No vigilantism on Bobrix. Decoy accounts and stings risk harm and compromise evidence. We provide built‑in, one‑click law‑enforcement referrals with secure evidence packaging.
  • Dedicated reporter program. Safety‑minded creators and investigators can enroll to submit leads via structured forms, encrypted uploads, and strict confidentiality rules. Submissions enter our triage queue (human+AI) with clear feedback loops.
  • Hard guardrails against off‑platform lures. Attempts to move chats to external apps are auto‑muted and flagged; guardians are notified for under‑13 accounts.
  • Independent oversight. An external Safety Council can review policies and audit high‑impact cases.
Why this matters

Reports in Aug 2025 about bans of vigilante creators (including “Schlep”) and related lawsuits spotlighted the need to channel community vigilance into safe, lawful, and effective pathways. That moment pushed us to commit fully to Bobrix and to design a reporter program from day one.

FAQ

Will my Roblox place convert perfectly? We’ll try to convert as much as possible (geometry, materials, properties, and common script patterns). Some features or custom modules may need manual tweaks. The importer will generate a compatibility report listing any issues.

How close is Luab to Roblox Lua/Luau? Very close. Familiar syntax and many mirrored APIs (e.g., services & events). Where names or behaviors differ, we’ll provide shims and deprecation warnings, plus a migration guide.
