Statements regarding the recent “Schlep” situation

Bobrix is not affiliated with Roblox. The following statements clarify our stance and how this situation motivated us to create Bobrix.
Our safety stance
  • No vigilantism on Bobrix. We prohibit decoy accounts and sting operations. Instead, we provide built‑in, one‑click law‑enforcement referrals with secure evidence packaging.
  • Dedicated reporter program. Safety‑minded members can apply to securely report bad actors, ensuring effective escalation without vigilante tactics.
  • Hard guardrails against off‑platform lures. Conversations attempting to move off‑platform are auto‑muted and flagged.
  • Independent oversight. An external Safety Council audits high‑impact enforcement decisions.
What happened elsewhere (Aug 2025)

Roblox removed vigilante creators, including “Schlep,” citing ToS violations related to impersonation of minors and moving users off‑platform. Several lawsuits alleged inadequate protection of minors. Roblox stated it partners with law enforcement and opposes vigilantism. This controversy motivated us to build Bobrix as a safer alternative.

How Bobrix is different
  • Evidence handling: report → preserve → escalate, without public exposure or vigilantism.
  • Strict age boundaries: enforced chat & matchmaking separation between minors and adults unless verified family/guardian.
  • Creator obligations: creators must adopt our Safety Toolkit (chat filters, session limits, reporting hooks) to publish.
  • Third‑party audits: external compliance reviews (NCMEC, COPPA, UK AADC).
